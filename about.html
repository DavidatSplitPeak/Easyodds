About Us

WHAT IS EASYODDS.COM?

Easyodds.com is a comprehensive betting service based in London that helps you find the best odds on offer from leading online bookmakers and betting exchanges and then helps you to place a bet at the best price on our website and our App. We also offer top betting tips from our team of leading industry experts plus plenty of competitions and promotions.


WHERE DID THE IDEA FOR EASYODDS.COM COME FROM?

When sports gambler Tony Plaskow won a first goalscorer bet at 7/1, he felt cheated when he discovered that another bookmaker was offering 10/1 on the same player. Guided by this sense of injustice, he teamed up with fellow punter David Gross to set up a service devoted to helping you find the best value for your bet. It was not long before the pioneering easyodds.com was born, on 4th July 2000, becoming the first sports betting site on the internet comparing real time betting odds from a range of online bookmakers on all major sports. Over the last 13 years we’ve built up a strong reputation for being one of the market leaders in the industry, with our truly independent service appreciated by punters who know they are getting the best value from their betting. Now led by CEO James Garmston, our achievements have also been recognised within the gaming industry and we were delighted to be nominated for ‘Best Overall Affiliate’ and 'Best Sportsbook Affiliate' at the 2013 iGB Awards. In addition, we were also named best odds comparison site for 2008, 2009, 2010 and 2011 as voted for by the readers of Gambling Online magazine. Furthermore, since 2010 we have featured in eGR’s Power 50 list, and as recently as this year (2015) we have been nominated for Best Affiliate at the EGR Awards which is a collec- tion of the most influential affiliates in the industry.
